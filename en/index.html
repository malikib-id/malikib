Why Code Quality and System Stability Are Crucial?

When launching new features or deploying a website to production, every second of system downtime poses a major risk to product stability and user experience:

  • User Churn: Users will immediately abandon the platform if buttons become unresponsive or APIs fail to load data efficiently.
  • Wasted Engineering Hours: Development teams lose valuable time panic-fixing critical bugs directly in production environment.
  • Disrupted Core Workflows: Any system failure along critical paths compromises data integrity and disrupts seamless transaction flows.

As digital products scale, relying solely on manual verification becomes a heavy bottleneck that drains the team's velocity. This is where automation steps in to handle the repetitive heavy lifting.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Automated Testing?

Automated testing is the process of verifying software applications using automated code scripts rather than manual human clicks. It executes testing scenarios exactly according to your defined conditions and requirements.

Is testing conducted directly on the live production website?

No, I do not recommend running tests directly in a production environment, as it risks disrupting active users. Testing should always be performed in a staging or development environment.

How is the pricing determined here?

Pricing is highly flexible and tailored to your convenience. Since the work is managed through Upwork, we can utilize either an hourly rate or a fixed-price per project system based on the feature workflows and test scenarios you require. Feel free to contact me to discuss the best option for your project.

Why use Automated Testing? What are the benefits?

Automated testing is crucial for maintaining fast deployment cycles without sacrificing software quality. The key benefits include:

  1. Time Efficiency: Tests that typically take hours to execute manually can be completed by scripts within minutes.
  2. High Accuracy: Eliminates human error by consistently checking the system with 100% precision every single run.
  3. Instant Bug Detection: Catches errors faster before the application reaches users, allowing the development team to fix them immediately.
What is the workflow and warranty policy for testing?

My workflow focuses on the stability of your feature paths before automation scripts are developed:

  1. Flow Validation Phase: I perform manual exploratory testing to ensure the feature flow is final and free of blocker bugs. If issues are found, I report them to your developers and offer a re-testing warranty based on our mutual agreement after fixes are deployed.
  2. Script Development Phase: Once the feature flow is stable (meaning business logic and page structures won't change drastically in the near future), I begin writing the automation scripts.
Why must feature workflows be stable before scripts are created?

Automation scripts follow precise, step-by-step actions on a website. If the development team introduces major workflow changes mid-way (such as altering button structures or skipping process pages), the automation scripts under development can become invalid. Ensuring flow stability upfront guarantees that your automation scripts remain highly accurate and long-lasting.
